About the Game

Pokémon TCG Live is the best way to learn how to play the Pokémon TCG at your own pace and at no cost. You’ll have access to every Pokémon TCG card currently available in the Standard format, Learning Lab tutorials to help you learn the rules, and a variety of decks that you can try after logging in. Pokémon TCG Live uses the same rules as the physical Pokémon TCG so everything you learn can be applied to in-person battles too!

Whether you’re looking to level up your skills for a friendly battle with your friend or your next in-person tournament, Pokémon TCG Live has you covered.

Game Modes

Pokémon TCG Live makes it easy to learn new skills and new strategies, all while enjoying the heat of battle online. You can battle in the Standard format featuring cards released over the last couple of years, fun Trainer Trials with varying rulesets to keep you on your toes and Build and Battle to emulate the experience of an in-person prerelease event. You can enjoy these game modes by using online matchmaking to battle against players across the world or challenge your friends at a whim in private battles.

Battle Pass

A new Battle Pass is available with the release of every new Pokémon TCG expansion!

Battle online and complete quests to unlock in-game cosmetics, booster packs, and more. Gain experience by battling and level up to earn more rewards.

The Pokémon TCG Live Battle Pass unlocks additional game items when you complete quests!

Build Your Card Collection

Add new cards to your deck or collection! Players can redeem codes from physical Pokémon TCG booster packs or earn booster packs from in-game expansion challenges. Players can also exchange in-game currency for individual cards, booster packs, and bundles.

Have a code card from a Pokémon TCG product? Scan or type in the code to redeem it.

Get Started

First-time Pokémon TCG Live players can begin by signing up for a Pokémon Trainer Club account. From there, download Pokémon TCG Live. When you log in the first time, the game will guide you through the setup process.
